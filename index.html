About AKILES

  • Established in 2022, AKILES Hauling Services is a trusted partner in logistics and transportation, offering reliable, secure, and punctual hauling services across Luzon. We are committed to supporting our clients' business growth through commitment and excellence in every delivery.
  • PEZA Accredited scrap buyer and duly registered business in the City of Cabuyao, Province of Laguna with Business License No. 2022-3512.
  • Founded by Engr Allan C. Neric, a licensed Mechanical Engineer with 30 years of experience working in the Philippines, Middle East and Australia. He assembled a team of professionals with supply chain and logistics background to provide end-to-end logistics needs of companies.
